Professional Summary

With over 20 years of experience in the software development industry, I've witnessed the evolution of numerous technologies and methodologies—adapting and contributing to each phase with enthusiasm and resilience. My journey began at Sysline, a small but agile company where I laid a strong technical foundation. The nimbleness of the organization enabled us to quickly adopt emerging tools and methodologies. It was here that my interest in Agile software development first took root, including early exposure to Extreme Programming (XP) principles.

I later joined Ixonos, where I contributed to cross-platform mobile development efforts—spanning Symbian, Linux, and Windows Phone ecosystems. Over time, I served in various capacities, from software developer to architect and team lead, driving innovation across different verticals and scaling both teams and systems.

Seeking a deeper technical role, I transitioned to Samlink, immersing myself in a new domain and embracing a developer-centric focus. This pivot reinvigorated my passion for hands-on problem-solving and technical excellence.

At Aidon, I serve as both Solution Architect and Platform Leader, spearheading initiatives that harness modern cloud-native paradigms. Our technology stack includes Microsoft Azure, Kubernetes, .NET Core and a suite of containerized microservices—each chosen to optimize scalability, resilience, and performance.

Now at Jamix , I continue to lead within the .NET and Azure cloud ecosystem, leveraging the latest in development tooling and architectural best practices. My focus remains on building scalable, maintainable, and intelligent systems—especially where AI can empower teams and transform how we work. A strong focus has also been placed on AI integration, enhancing operational intelligence and accelerating productivity across products and processes.
